The Earth Feeds Us, Let's Honor It's Gift

Connect food donors with NGOs to ensure excess food reaches those in need instead of landfills.

We're on a mission to reduce food waste by connecting those with surplus food to those who need it most, creating a more sustainable and equitable food system.

Reduce Food Waste

Approximately one-third of food produced globally is wasted. We're working to change that.

Feed The Hungry

Connect surplus food with local NGOs and community organization that serve those in need

Environmental Impact

Reducing food waste means less methane emission from landfills and conservation of resources

How It Works

1

Register

Sign up as donor (restaurant, canteen, event planner) or recipient (NGO, food bank).

2

Connect

Donors post available food, and recipient browse and request donations in area.

3

Distribute

Coordinate pickup or delivery of food to ensure it reaches those who need it most.
